Privacy Policy — BabySoma

Last updated: January 2025

BabySoma (“we”, “our”, “us”) is a mobile application designed to help parents track their baby’s sleep, routines, and development. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Information You Provide
- Baby’s name, age, date of birth
- Sleep logs, feeding logs, routines
- Notes you enter manually
- Email used for Firebase Authentication

1.2. Automatically Collected Information
We use Firebase to collect: device info, app usage, crash logs. Firebase does not collect sensitive baby information.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the data to provide app features, analyze sleep, show statistics, sync data, and improve performance.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). We never sell or share your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data except when required by law or for security (crash logs).

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion or export of your data via email: support@approvex.io

6. Children’s Privacy

BabySoma is intended for parents, not for children. We do not collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The latest version will always be available on this page.
